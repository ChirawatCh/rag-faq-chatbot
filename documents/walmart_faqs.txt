Walmart+ Frequently Asked Questions

Walmart+ Membership (categories)


-----------------------

What is Walmart+?

Walmart+ is Walmart’s membership program helping members save more time & money with exclusive benefits. With Walmart+, you get free delivery on groceries & more, gas savings, video streaming + so much more!

* * *

What is included in a Walmart+ plan?

As a Walmart+ member, you’ll enjoy benefits including free delivery from your store, free shipping with no order minimum, member prices on fuel at select stations, Burger King savings, video streaming with Paramount+, Scan & Go, limited-time offers & more. We’re always working on adding even more great benefits, too!

* * *

What types of Walmart+ plans are available?

Monthly: $12.95/month. Annual: $98/year. Plus, applicable taxes. If you’re eligible for government assistance or a college student, you may be eligible for 50% off your membership. To learn more about Walmart+ Assist please visit the [Walmart+ Assist page](https://www.walmart.com/plus/assist). Student discounts are also offered seasonally. You may also be eligible to receive a free 30-day trial. [Learn more here](https://www.walmart.com/plus).

* * *

Can I switch to a different Walmart+ plan after I sign up?

Yes. You can switch plans from monthly to annual in the [Walmart+ membership hub](https://www.walmart.com/account/plus/manage). If you’re eligible for government assistance, you may be eligible to switch your plan to receive a 50% discount. [Learn more here](https://www.walmart.com/plus/assist).

* * *

Do I need to download the Walmart app to use Walmart+?

No, you don’t need the app to use Walmart+. However, to maximize your experience, we recommend downloading the app to receive full access to all your benefits, like member prices on fuel.

* * *

Where can I manage my Walmart+ account?

On your Walmart+ [account page](https://www.walmart.com/account/plus/manage).

* * *

Who do I contact for assistance with my Walmart+ account?

If you need assistance, please visit our [help center](https://www.walmart.com/help).

Walmart+ Assist (categories)


-------------------

What is Walmart+ Assist?

Walmart+ Assist is a program where eligible customers can join Walmart+ to enjoy free delivery from store, free shipping with no order minimums, video streaming & all the other Walmart+ benefits at 50% off the regular membership price. This program is offered to those who qualify for government assistance programs & can provide sufficient documents to prove their eligibility (if required). Walmart+ Assist members receive 50% off membership, giving them all benefits of Walmart+ for only $6.47/month or $49/year.  
Eligible programs include SNAP, WIC, Medicaid, SSI, TANF, TTANF, NSLP & LIHEAP.  
To learn more & check if you qualify, [click here](https://www.walmart.com/plus/assist).

* * *

Does Walmart+ Assist offer a free trial?

Yes. People who’ve never been a Walmart+ member before & are successfully verified will get a free 30-day trial to start their membership.

* * *

How is Walmart+ Assist different from a full price Walmart+ Membership?

Walmart+ Assist members enjoy every membership benefit that full price Walmart+ members enjoy—just at a discounted rate of $6.47/month or $49/year.

* * *

Which membership types are eligible for Walmart+ Assist?

New members, existing members, canceled members, paused members & trial members are all eligible for Walmart+ Assist. To transfer your membership from Walmart+ to Walmart+ Assist, [click here](https://www.walmart.com/plus/assist) & complete the Walmart+ Assist sign-up process.  
Note: If your Walmart+ membership is associated with a service you receive through another company (i.e., Amex, TracFone, etc.), you’ll need to end that association before you can sign up for Walmart+ Assist.

* * *

How will I be verified for eligibility to enroll into the Walmart+ Assist discounted membership?

If Walmart is unable to verify your eligibility, Walmart+ has partnered with a third-party verification provider called “SheerID” to verify your eligibility for Walmart+ Assist. If required as part of the membership enrollment process, you’ll be taken to the SheerID website where you’ll be asked to fill out the eligibility verification form. Upon successful completion of verification, you’ll be asked to return to the Walmart site or app continue with your Walmart+ Assist enrollment.

* * *

What is Sheer ID?

SheerID is a third-party verification service that works on behalf of retailers to verify an individual’s affiliations. For information on their services, visit them at [SheerID.com](https://www.sheerid.com/). For additional support with SheerID, visit the [SheerID Help Center](https://support.sheerid.com/en-US/6499bff71bd6276caa07a88a/help-center/lowincome).

* * *

What qualifying documents do I need to provide to verify my eligibility?

You may only need to show documents if your initial verification fails. SheerID will let you know what specific documents you need to upload to prove eligibility.

* * *

What happens if I can’t prove that I am eligible during membership enrollment?

If you cannot provide acceptable proof of eligibility, we’ll be unable to approve you for Walmart+ Assist. You’ll still be eligible for a full price Walmart+ membership, which you can sign up for at [Walmart.com/Plus](https://www.walmart.com/plus). For additional support with SheerID, visit the [SheerID Help Center](https://support.sheerid.com/en-US/6499bff71bd6276caa07a88a/help-center/lowincome).

* * *

How long will my verification be valid?

Your verified status will be valid for one year from the start date of your paid Walmart+ Assist membership. Walmart+ will verify your eligibility for the program on an annual basis in an offline process without customer input for most cases.

* * *

What happens if my annual re-verification fails?

If your annual re-verification fails, you’ll no longer be eligible for a Walmart+ Assist membership. Your plan will auto-renew for $12.95/month or $98/year plus applicable taxes until you cancel your membership. You’ll be notified via email about the changes to your membership plan. For additional support with SheerID, visit the [SheerID Help Center](https://support.sheerid.com/en-US/6499bff71bd6276caa07a88a/help-center/lowincome).

Pause Membership (categories)


--------------------

What does “Pause membership” mean?

You can pause your membership to avoid being billed for one month. You’ll also lose access to your benefits during this paused period. Your membership will be paused at the end of your current billing cycle. Annual members can also pause their membership, which will begin at the end of the billing cycle.

* * *

How does it work?

When you pause your membership, you won’t be charged during the paused period. You also won’t be able to use your membership benefits from the time their current billing cycle ends until the pause period ends.

* * *

How many times can a member pause their membership?

A member can pause their membership for one month at a time & up to three times in a 12-month rolling period.

* * *

When can a member pause their membership?

Members can pause their membership at any time in their current billing cycle. If they pause their membership in the middle of a billing cycle, it will go into effect at the end of their current billing cycle.

* * *

Who is eligible to pause membership?

Currently only paid annual & monthly members can pause their membership. If you’re on a discounted plan such as Walmart+ Assist, you’ll need to reverify your eligibility each year after your sign-up.

* * *

What membership benefits & functions are inaccessible during a paused membership?

You’ll lose access to all of your benefits during the paused state. You’ll have full access again once your membership is resumed.

Is there a limit to how many times I can order?

No, you can order as often as you want!

* * *

What types of shipping qualify for Walmart+ free shipping?

* * *

Can I place an order with free shipping & free delivery in the same order?

Yes! You can order your groceries, shop for your home & more—all at the same time. Items sold & shipped by Walmart will be shipped free with no order minimum needed. Items from your local store will be delivered free during the time slot you choose & your order needs to meet the $35 order minimum.

Burger King® savings (categories)


------------------------

Who is eligible to redeem this benefit?

Active trial & paid Walmart+ members.

* * *

What is the Burger King® savings benefit?

Walmart+ members can get 25% off one order per day on the BK® App or bk.com with no order minimum. Members can also get a free Whopper® with any purchase every 3 months with no order minimum. This offer is available for redemption one time in 2024. Starting 2025, members can redeem this offer the first day of January, April, July & October. It expires at the end of each 3-month period. Unredeemed offers won’t carry over into the next period. Both offers cannot be combined with a Your Way Meal deal.

* * *

How do I use this benefit?

Just link your Walmart+ membership with a new or existing BK® Royal Perks account.

* * *

How do I link my Walmart+ membership to a BK® Royal Perks account?

Go to your [Walmart+ member hub](https://www.walmart.com/account/plus/overview), navigate to “Burger King® savings,” click “Get started” then select “Get started now” on the benefit page. You’ll be routed to the BK® App or website where you’ll need to create a new BK® Royal Perks account using your Walmart+ credentials or link your existing BK® Royal Perks account by clicking “Link with a different account.” Once linked, you can go to the “Offers” tab on the BK® App or website & select an offer.

* * *

Where can I claim this benefit?

To get 25% off one order per calendar day, you’ll need to place a mobile order via the BK® App or website available for pickup in-restaurant, drive-through or delivery with an order minimum of $5 (excl. taxes) or curbside pickup where available. This offer can’t be redeemed in-restaurant orders. To get a free Whopper® with any purchase every 3 months, you’ll need to place a mobile order or redeem in-restaurant with your unique 6-digit Royal Perks ID, which can be found in the “My code” tab on the BK® App or website.

* * *

Can I redeem both offers at the same time?

No, offers can’t be combined. You’ll have to wait at least one hour to redeem both offers on the same day.

* * *

What are the limitations of this benefit?

Both offers are valid at participating restaurants in all states except Hawaii & Alaska. Offers can’t be redeemed at restaurants outside the U.S. or through any third-party site. Both offers are not valid with any other coupons or offers & can’t be combined for the same order. The 25% discount is available only when ordering through the BK® App or website.

* * *

Can I share my benefit with friends & family?

This benefit can only be redeemed by Walmart+ members, but we encourage you to treat yourself or the whole family when placing your order!

Which devices can I use to watch Paramount+?

Stream Paramount+ on your computer, mobile device, smart TV or connected device.

How do I cancel my Paramount+ with SHOWTIME plan?

Member prices on fuel (categories)


-------------------------

What are “member prices on fuel” & how do I get them?

Using the Walmart app, Walmart+ members will receive 10¢ off per gallon at Exxon & Mobil stations (5¢ in AL & OK), up to 10¢ off at select Walmart & Murphy stations & member prices at Sam’s Club fuel centers.

* * *

How do I access member prices on fuel at the pump?

There are three ways to access the benefit in the Walmart app:  
1\. Select “Member prices on fuel” on your Walmart+ membership page & follow the on-screen instructions.  
2\. Go to the “Services” tab, select “Member prices on fuel” & follow the instructions.  
3\. Open the universal scanner in the search bar on the home screen & scan the QR code at the pump to get started.

* * *

How do I pay for member prices on fuel?

At Exxon & Mobil fuel stations, you can only pay at the pump & must use the credit/debit card linked to your existing Walmart+ account. At Murphy stations, you can pay at the pump using your credit/debit card or pay a cashier inside the fuel station using cash or a credit/debit card. At Walmart or Sam’s Club Fuel stations, you can pay at the pump using Walmart Pay, or pay an Associate inside the fuel station using cash or a credit/debit card.

* * *

Can I receive member prices on fuel & pay in cash?

Yes. You can prepay with cash, debit or credit card & receive member prices on fuel at all Walmart & Murphy fuel stations. If you’re at a Walmart fuel station, provide your barcode to the Associate inside the station. Just click “Pay with cash instead” when accessing the benefit in the Walmart app & the barcode will be generated. If you’re at a Murphy station, provide your 6-digit ID to the cashier inside the station. The ID can be found in the Walmart app while at the Murphy station, or by navigating (in-app) to Account \> See all your Walmart+ benefits \> Member prices on fuel \> Start fueling.

* * *

What if I forget to bring my Walmart app to the pump?

Your member prices on fuel can’t be applied without the app.

* * *

Is there a limit to how many gallons I can get member prices on fuel?

No. There’s no limit to how many gallons you can get with member prices on fuel.

* * *

Can I access member prices on fuel more than once per day?

Yes. You’ll receive member prices on fuel each time you fill up.

* * *

Can member prices on fuel be applied to all fuel types?

Yes. All fuel types qualify.

* * *

If I can’t figure it out at the pump, can an attendant help me?

Yes. An attendant can help you complete your transaction.

What is mobile scan & go?

With mobile scan & go, you can shop & checkout with your phone in stores. It’ll save you time & it’s contact-free. Just scan, pay & be on your way.

* * *

How do I get started with mobile scan & go?

First, be sure to allow Location Access when you open the Walmart app for the first time (if you have previously not allowed this, you’ll need to update it in your phone’s settings). Then, open the Walmart app when you’re inside a Walmart store & select mobile scan & go. Simply scan items as you add them to your cart before checking out & paying directly from your phone.

* * *

Do I need to download the Walmart app to use scan & go?

Yes, you’ll need the Walmart app to use mobile scan & go.

* * *

Do all Walmart locations offer scan & go?

Yes, mobile scan & go works in all Walmart locations.

* * *

Do I need to be connected to the store’s WiFi to use scan & go?

No, but make sure that your location services are on for the app, so you get access to our virtual assistant to use scan & go, Walmart Pay, item finder & other services.

* * *

Is there a limit to how many things I can buy in one trip using scan & go?

Nope! You can use scan & go whether buying one item or several carts’ full.

* * *

Can I get Walmart Cash with scan & go?

Yes, you can redeem manufacturer offers on eligible items when using scan & go. In the app, be sure to check the offer box for each eligible item you’re purchasing. If you’ve met the offer requirements, you’ll get Walmart Cash upon successful checkout.

* * *

What payment methods can I use with scan & go?

You can add payment methods including major credit & debit cards or update your payment preferences in [your Walmart account](https://www.walmart.com/account).

* * *

Do I need to show my receipt to anyone before leaving the store?

Walmart+ members must go through self-checkout & scan the QR code provided in the app. You’ll then get your receipt & an Associate will be able to check your ID for age-restricted items, such as alcohol.

* * *

How do I bag items I purchased using scan & go?

If you have reusable bags, you can bag your items as you shop, or you can bag your items at self-checkout when you scan your QR code & get your receipt.

Walmart+ Travel (categories)


-------------------

What is Walmart+ Travel?

Walmart+ Travel makes every trip more rewarding! Walmart+ members get up to 5% Walmart Cash when booking a trip through [Walmart+ Travel](https://www.walmartplustravel.com/), powered by Expedia. Members earn 2% Walmart Cash on airfare & 5% Walmart Cash on hotel bookings, vacation & car rentals, tours, activities & more. Members must book travel through WalmartPlusTravel.com. Non-members can book travel through Walmart+ Travel as well but won’t get Walmart Cash.  
Walmart Cash will appear as pending in your ledger until your trip is completed. You’ll be able to redeem pending Walmart Cash 30 days after the completion of your trip or reservation.

* * *

Where can I find my booking(s)?

Check your email inbox for confirmation sent by Expedia. Additionally, you can access your booking(s) by going to [WalmartPlusTravel.com](https://www.walmartplustravel.com/) & selecting Trips.

* * *

How is the Walmart Cash calculated when booking vacation packages?

If you’re booking a combination of air travel & other accommodations, you will receive 2% Walmart Cash on your airfare & 5% Walmart Cash on hotel bookings, vacation & car rentals, tours, activities & more.

* * *

What happens to my Walmart Cash if I cancel or modify my reservation(s)?

The Walmart Cash you receive with the corresponding booking will be adjusted in your “pending” Walmart Cash balance within 24–48 hours in your account.

* * *

Will I get Walmart Cash through Walmart+ Travel powered by Expedia if I cancel my membership?

No. You must be a current Walmart+ member at the time of booking to receive Walmart Cash. You cannot receive Walmart Cash retroactively.

* * *

Does Walmart Cash expire?

No, your Walmart Cash never expires.

* * *

Can I redeem my Walmart Cash on future bookings through Walmart+ Travel powered by Expedia?

No, you can use your Walmart Cash on future Walmart purchases or redeem it for cash back. See [Walmart Cash FAQ](https://www.walmart.com/help/article/walmart-rewards-faqs/3260c30df45443de80b4ac720717af28) for questions on restrictions for redeeming Walmart Cash.

* * *

When will I see my Walmart Cash received through Walmart+ Travel in my account?

You’ll see your Walmart Cash as “pending” in your account until your travel is complete. The final amount of Walmart Cash you received will be reflected in your account 30 days after the completion of your trip or reservation.

* * *

Will the Walmart Cash received through Walmart+ Travel be combined with Walmart Cash from other programs?

Yes. All the Walmart Cash that you receive will be pooled into one balance & can be redeemed on future purchases in Walmart stores, Walmart.com or redeemed for cash back at any Walmart store. See [Walmart Cash FAQ](https://www.walmart.com/help/article/walmart-rewards-faqs/3260c30df45443de80b4ac720717af28) for questions on restrictions for receiving Walmart Cash.

* * *

If I have any questions, changes or issues with my trip or bookings, who can I contact?

Contact Walmart+ Travel customer service, powered by Expedia at +1(800)323-3897 (toll free) or +1(312)279-7755 (international toll) for additional assistance with your reservations. You can also contact the airline directly for specific questions regarding your flight. For questions about your Walmart Cash balance, you can reach us at [Walmart.com/Help](https://www.walmart.com/help).

* * *

Why am I not seeing the correct amount of Walmart Cash reflected on the Walmart+ Travel site (WalmartPlusTravel.com) or in my Walmart.com account?

If you are on [WalmartPlusTravel.com](https://www.walmartplustravel.com/) & not seeing Walmart Cash for your bookings, please log out & log back in. If you are not seeing the accurate pending Walmart Cash on your [Walmart.com](https://www.walmart.com/) (Walmart+) account, please wait at least 48 hours after making your booking for this to be updated.

I’m already a Walmart+ Member, how do I activate this benefit?

Go to your [Walmart+ membership hub](https://www.walmart.com/account/plus/overview) & find the Pawp benefit (“Free online pet care”). Select “Get started” & you’ll be prompted to create a Pawp account. This benefit is subject to Pawp’s Terms of Service found at [Pawp.com/tos](https://www.walmart.com/plus/Pawp.com/tos).

* * *

How much is Pawp with Walmart+?

Pawp is included with your Walmart+ monthly or annual membership at no additional cost. Walmart+ members who activate the benefit will save $99 on the cost of a standalone Pawp membership. As an optional add-on, Pawp also offers Pawp members its Emergency Fund protection for $19 per month. This gives you access to up to $3,000/year in a pet emergency.

* * *

How do I get $3,000 of Emergency Protection?

Upon sign-up you’ll be able to review information related to Pawp’s Emergency Fund protection & be offered the option to add the service. The cost for Emergency Fund protection is $19 per month & you’ll be billed by Pawp directly.

* * *

I’m not currently a Walmart+ member, but can I sign up for Walmart+ to get access to Pawp?

Yes! Visit [Walmart/com/Plus](https://www.walmart.com/plus/walmart.com/plus) to join Walmart+. Once you become a Walmart+ member, visit your [account page](https://www.walmart.com/account), click on the Pawp benefit & follow the steps to create an account.

* * *

Can I use Pawp on both mobile & desktop?

No. The Pawp service is only available on mobile devices. When signing up, you’ll be directed to download the Pawp app to utilize the benefit services.

What’s Walmart+ InHome?

InHome is available in select locations & is an upgrade to your Walmart+ membership. InHome is an enhanced, tip-free delivery service using local Walmart Associates to deliver your order where & how you want it—even inside your garage or fridge! To learn more or sign up, can [click here](https://www.walmart.com/plus/inhome). To learn more about billing, payments or other information you can visit the InHome FAQ page [here](https://www.walmart.com/plus/inhome-frequently-asked-questions).

* * *

What's included with Walmart+ InHome?

In addition to all the Walmart+ benefits, with InHome you also enjoy delivery where you want it whether in your garage, kitchen (even into your fridge) or to your doorstep. InHome deliveries are done by familiar, local Walmart Associates. There are no added fees & tips are included with every delivery. With InHome, you also get easy, free return pickup if needed.

* * *

What InHome plans are available & how much are they?

It’s $40/year for an annual InHome upgrade in addition to your annual Walmart+ membership fee, plus applicable taxes. A monthly InHome upgrade is also available for $7/month in addition to your monthly Walmart+ membership fee, plus applicable taxes.

Can I get InHome without having a Walmart+ membership?
